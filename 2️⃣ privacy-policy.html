Privacy Policy for chandini

This Privacy Policy explains how chandini handles user data when using the YouTube Data API v3. Your privacy is important and all data accessed is strictly for operational purposes.

Information Collected

chandini collects OAuth access tokens and basic YouTube account information necessary to upload and manage videos. No additional personal information is requested.

Use of Information

  • Upload videos to YouTube
  • Manage video metadata
  • Set video visibility and publishing status

Data Storage

Access tokens are stored securely in our database. chandini does not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties.

Third-Party Services

chandini uses Google APIs and complies with Google Privacy Policy.

User Control

Users can revoke access to chandini at any time via their Google account security settings.
